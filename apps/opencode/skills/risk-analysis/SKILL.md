# Risk Analysis

Use this skill when the user asks for risk review, assessment or analysis.

Return:

1. Risk register (risk, likelihood, impact, severity)
2. Categorized by area (technical, organizational, financial, timeline)
3. Mitigation strategies for each risk
4. Risk owners if identifiable
5. Residual risk after mitigation
6. Recommended actions

Rate likelihood and impact as low/medium/high. Be specific, not generic.
